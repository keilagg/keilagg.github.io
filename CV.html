Ph.D. candidate and researcher at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid and Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo. My current research focuses on the application of geospatial data to road design and safety analyses. My goal is to improve the knowledge of how new trends in the acquisition of spatial data could help transportation issues and road safety in general. In addition to that, I am interested in the benefits that neocartography could bring to common challenges faced by developing countries. Skills: data analysis; GIS; research; Bicycle Facilities.
