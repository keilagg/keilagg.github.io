Conferences

I have had the opportunity to participate in several conferences during my academic formation. Most of these publications were related to my research on road safety and geospatial technologies. During my postdoctoral research at Transyt-UPM, my research was motivated by equity concerns in road pricing. Those related to my research at UASD focus on neocartography.
